Explainable AI refers to methods and techniques in the application of artificial intelligence technology such that the results of the solution can be understood by human experts. Standard machine learning models are "black boxes" — they give a prediction but not the reasoning. LoanIQ uses SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values to make the model transparent. It decomposes the final score to show exactly how much each feature (like your debt ratio or credit score) pushed your approval probability up or down.

Our system uses a trained Machine Learning model (Random Forest Classifier) that analyzes historical loan data. It evaluates 15 key features including DTI Ratio, Credit Score, Savings, Age, Applicant Income, and Loan Purpose. The prediction threshold is set at 45% probability; if the model assesses your chance at 45% or higher, your loan is predicted as Approved.
